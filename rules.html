While Modern lets you play cards from as far back as 2003,Vintage lets you play cards from any set in the 20+ year history of Magic, UX let's you play even more!

The following cards are banned from Vintage UX play:

  • 9 cards that reference "playing for ante." Click here for list.

  • Falling Star
  • Lurrus of the Dream-Den
  • Shahrazad
